Merge sort works with the divide and conquer strategy. It divides the array into subarrays until it's a unit array and then it merges them in the sorted order. It essentially builds the array back up in the direction it was divided into and this is a vast improvement with a time complexity of O(N*log(N)) with a space complexity of O(N). Merge Sort is used in todays world to sort any large dataset.
